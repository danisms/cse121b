Introduction

Interactive Tic Tac Toe is a single-player computer game.
The computer is built to challenge players in competitive games, aiming to improve concentration and logical reasoning skills.

How To Play

The player selects a difficulty level from the available options (Easy, Medium, Hard). Each level of difficulty represents the level of reasoning that the machine employs while interacting with the player. Hard, requiring a high level of concentration and reasoning to win.

In the first round, the player makes the starting move, followed by the computer. If the game ends without a winner, the first move is alternated, with the computer making the first move and vice versa. However, if there is a winner, the winner takes the first move in the second round.

Every time the player wins, the computer delivers a joke as a form of reward. Interestingly, for every loss by the player, the computer provides an inspirational quote as a form of motivation.

Note

To end the game, click the pause button at the top of the screen, located between the player and computer score trackers.

In addition to the ending the game with the pause button, the game prompts players to either end or continue playing after each win or loss.
